Help & Information
About Absences

When a trainer cannot attend a session, you need to record their absence. If possible, assign a replacement trainer.

Absence Reasons
  • Medical: Health issues, medical appointments
  • Personal: Family events, emergencies
  • Professional: Other work commitments
  • Other: Any reason not in the above categories
Important Notes
  • For planned absences, record them as soon as possible
  • A justification document is recommended for medical absences
  • Always try to find a replacement trainer when possible
Replacement Status

The absence status is determined by whether a replacement trainer has been assigned:

Approved A replacement trainer has been assigned and confirmed
Pending Waiting for confirmation or looking for a replacement
Rejected No replacement could be found, or request was denied
